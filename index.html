🏴 Scotland

Research by Kai

Gain international experience while exploring one of Europe's most beautiful countries. Scotland offers amazing nature, welcoming people and meaningful volunteering opportunities.

🏔 Nature

Explore the Highlands, Loch Ness, the Isle of Skye and Scotland's breathtaking national parks.

🏰 Culture

Visit historic castles, discover Scottish traditions and experience the unique local culture.

🤝 Community

Support charities, environmental organisations and local communities while improving your English.

Requirements

📄 Documents

• Valid passport

• Charity Worker Visa

• Sponsorship by a registered charity

🗣 Language

Basic English skills are recommended to communicate with colleagues and local people.

💷 Financial Proof

Applicants may need proof of financial maintenance unless the sponsoring charity confirms financial support.

Estimated Costs

Charity Worker Visa £379
Immigration Health Surcharge £1,035
Travel Insurance £200–£500
Flights £100–£300
Total £1,714–£2,214

🏴 England

Research by Anna

England offers countless volunteering opportunities in one of Europe's most diverse countries. From historic cities to beautiful countryside, volunteers can gain valuable experience while making a positive impact.

🏙 Famous Cities

Visit London, Manchester, Liverpool, Birmingham and many other exciting cities full of history, culture and modern attractions.

🏛 Heritage

Help preserve museums, historical buildings, castles and cultural heritage sites throughout England.

🌱 Volunteering

Support environmental projects, youth organisations, charities, animal shelters and community programmes.

Why Choose England?

🎓 Education

Improve your English every day while gaining practical work experience that can strengthen your future career.

🌍 Diversity

Meet people from all over the world and experience one of Europe's most multicultural countries.

💼 Career Benefits

Develop teamwork, communication and problem-solving skills that are valuable for university and future jobs.

Typical Volunteer Projects

🌳 Nature

Tree planting, park maintenance, wildlife conservation and environmental education.

👨‍👩‍👧 Community

Support food banks, youth clubs, local charities and community events.

🐶 Animal Care

Volunteer at animal shelters, rescue centres or wildlife organisations.

🏴 Wales

Research by Finja

Wales is a fantastic destination for volunteers who love nature, wildlife and local communities. From mountains to the coastline, Wales offers unforgettable experiences and meaningful projects.

🏔 Eryri National Park

Explore one of the UK's most beautiful national parks (formerly Snowdonia). Volunteers help protect nature, hiking trails and wildlife.

🌊 Coastline

Support beach clean-ups, marine conservation and coastal protection projects around the Welsh coast.

🏰 Welsh Heritage

Visit famous castles like Caernarfon Castle and experience Wales' unique culture, language and traditions.

Volunteer Opportunities

🌿 Keep Wales Tidy

Join environmental projects that improve parks, forests and public spaces while protecting local wildlife.

🦭 Marine Life Rescue

Help protect seals, seabirds and marine ecosystems together with local conservation organisations.

🤝 Community Projects

Support local charities, youth organisations and cultural events while improving your English skills.

Accommodation

🏡 Housing

Depending on the organisation, accommodation may be provided. Other volunteers stay in hostels, shared houses or with host families.

🍽 Daily Life

Many charities support volunteers with meals, local transport or helpful information during their stay.

💷 Estimated Costs

Visa: £379
Immigration Health Surcharge: £1,035
Flights: £100–£300
Insurance: £200–£500
